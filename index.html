Which service might be right for you?

Coaching and therapy are genuinely different kinds of support — here is a quick way to think about which might suit you better.

ADHD coaching might suit you if…

  • You want practical strategies for managing life with ADHD
  • You are ready to make changes and want support with goals
  • You want tools and structures you can put into action
  • You want to understand how to work with your brain

Psychotherapy might suit you if…

  • You want to explore past experiences and patterns
  • You feel stuck but find it hard to name exactly why
  • You want to understand yourself rather than solve a problem
  • Questions of identity or relationships feel central

Not sure which is right for you? That is exactly what the free call is for. I will help you think it through — and I will always be honest if I think something else might serve you better. You can also read more about both services.

Could this be for you?

You might benefit from ADHD coaching if you are…

  • ✦   Struggling with time blindness and deadlines
  • ✦   Feeling constantly overwhelmed or disorganised
  • ✦   Difficulty starting tasks (even ones you want to do)
  • ✦   Emotional dysregulation and rejection sensitivity
  • ✦   Ready to make a change but unsure where to start

You might benefit from psychotherapy if you are…

  • ✦   Going around in circles with the same thoughts or patterns
  • ✦   Carrying something from the past that still gets in the way
  • ✦   Struggling with anxiety, low mood, or a sense of not quite knowing yourself
  • ✦   Looking for a space to be heard — without an agenda or advice
  • ✦   Wanting to understand yourself better, even if you cannot name exactly what is wrong

You do not need to have everything figured out before you reach out. That is exactly what the free call is for.

How it works

Free call

We start with a free, no-obligation 20-minute call so we can get to know each other and make sure coaching is the right fit.

Book your sessions

Each session lasts one hour, in person or online. Coaching can be booked as a block or pay as you go; psychotherapy is booked session by session.

Work together

We meet regularly to explore, plan, and problem-solve — always at your pace and focused on your goals.

Keep moving forward

Between sessions you have the tools and support to put things into practice, with the option to continue after each block.
