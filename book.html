A relaxed 20-minute chat — no commitment, no pressure, no prior preparation needed.

What is a discovery call?

A discovery call is a free, informal 20-minute conversation — by phone or video — where we can get to know each other a little before you decide whether coaching feels right for you.

There is no assessment, no selling, and no obligation to book anything afterwards. It is simply a chance to talk, ask questions, and get a feel for how we might work together.

What we might cover

  • What has brought you to look into ADHD coaching
  • What you are hoping to get out of coaching
  • How the sessions work and what to expect
  • Any questions you have for me
  • Whether we feel like a good fit

Prefer to email first? That is completely fine too. You can reach me at hello@puzzlepeace.co.uk and we can arrange a call from there.
