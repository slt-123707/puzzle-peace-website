Get in touch

What happens next?

You email me

Just say hello and tell me a little about what has brought you here. There is no need to have all the answers — a few sentences is absolutely fine.

I reply within two working days

I will read your email personally and get back to you with a warm response. I may have a couple of questions, or I may suggest booking a discovery call.

We have a free discovery call

A relaxed, 20-minute chat — by phone or video — to get to know each other. No commitment required. You can ask me anything you like.

You decide

If coaching feels like the right fit, we book your first block. If it does not, that is completely fine too — there is never any pressure.

A note before you reach out

Getting in touch can feel like a big step — especially if you have spent years feeling like you should be able to manage everything on your own.

You do not need to have a clear goal in mind. You do not need to have the right words. You do not need to justify why you are reaching out.

Just say hello. I will take it from there.
