What happens next?

Give me a call or send a text to arrange a time to chat. There is no need to have all the answers — a few words is absolutely fine.

Your privacy & GDPR

When you call me, any personal information you choose to share will be held in confidence. If we go on to work together, I may keep brief session notes to support our work.

I will use this information only to support our coaching or psychotherapy relationship. I will never share your information with third parties for marketing purposes, and I will never sell your data.

Your data is processed on the basis of legitimate interests (to respond to your enquiry) or, where you become a client, to fulfil our contract. Client records are retained for 7 years after the end of our relationship.

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. To exercise any of your rights, or if you have concerns about how your data is handled, please see the full Privacy Policy.

Puzzle Peace ADHD Coaching and Psychotherapy is registered with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO). Registration number: ZC033803.

A note before you reach out

Getting in touch can feel like a big step — especially if you have spent years feeling like you should be able to manage everything on your own.

You do not need to have a clear goal in mind. You do not need to have the right words. You do not need to justify why you are reaching out.

Just say hello. I will take it from there.

— Maggie
