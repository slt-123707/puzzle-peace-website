Two ways I can support you

I offer two distinct one-to-one services. Both take place in person in NG11 or online via Zoom, and both begin with a free 20-minute call.

🧠

ADHD Coaching

A practical, forward-focused partnership for adults with ADHD. Coaching is about building strategies, developing tools, and working towards your goals — in a way that fits how your brain actually works.

🌿

Psychotherapy

One-to-one person-centred therapy — a space to slow down, look inward, and explore the experiences and patterns that have shaped who you are. Open to anyone, not only those with ADHD.

Meet Maggie

Maggie is a qualified Integrative Psychotherapist and ADHD coach based in Gotham, Nottingham, with over 20 years of experience as a UKCP accredited therapist, supervisor and tutor.

After her own ADHD diagnosis aged 51, and her daughter's diagnosis of ASC and ADHD, Maggie found very little support available for late-diagnosed women and neurodivergent families. Puzzle Peace grew from that experience — a service shaped around the way neurodivergent brains actually work.

Sessions with Maggie are warm, non-judgemental, and always led by what matters most to you.

How it works

Free call

We start with a free, no-obligation 20-minute call so we can get to know each other and make sure coaching is the right fit.

Book your sessions

Each session lasts one hour, in person or online. Coaching can be booked as a block or pay as you go; psychotherapy is booked session by session.

Work together

We meet regularly to explore, plan, and problem-solve — always at your pace and focused on your goals.

Keep moving forward

Between sessions you have the tools and support to put things into practice, with the option to continue after each block.
