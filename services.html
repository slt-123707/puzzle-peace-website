Services & Pricing

Everything you need to know about how coaching works and what to expect.

One-to-one ADHD coaching

All coaching at Puzzle Peace is one-to-one and tailored entirely to you. There are no scripts or generic programmes — just a focused, collaborative space built around your goals, your challenges, and the way your brain works.

Session format

Each session lasts 50 minutes and takes place in person at [location — e.g. my practice in X town / a private room at X]. The space is quiet, comfortable, and easy to get to.

Sessions are focused but relaxed. You do not need to prepare anything in advance — just bring yourself and whatever is on your mind.

Block booking

Sessions are sold in blocks of six, paid for in advance. Block booking allows us to build real momentum together, and it gives you a committed period of time to work on your goals.

Sessions are typically scheduled once a week or fortnight, depending on what suits you. We will agree a schedule at the start of your block.

At the end of each block, you can choose to book a further block or step back and continue using the tools we have built together — it is entirely your decision.

Payment

Payment is required in advance for each block of six sessions. This secures your time in the diary. Payment details will be confirmed when you book.

I accept [payment methods — e.g. bank transfer / card / etc.]. If you have a question about payment or need to discuss your circumstances, please do get in touch — I am always happy to have an honest conversation.

Cancellation policy

I ask for at least 48 hours' notice if you need to cancel or rearrange a session. Sessions cancelled with less notice cannot be refunded, though I will always try to find a way to reschedule where possible. Life happens — please just let me know as soon as you can.

Free discovery call

Before you commit to a block, I offer a free 20-minute discovery call by phone or video. This is a chance for us to have an informal chat, for you to ask any questions, and for both of us to get a sense of whether we are a good fit. There is absolutely no pressure or obligation.

What does coaching cover?

Every coaching journey is different, but here are some of the areas we might explore together:

⏰

Time & planning

Managing time blindness, building routines that stick, and getting a handle on deadlines and prioritisation.

🚀

Getting started

Understanding task paralysis and procrastination, and finding the strategies that help you take that first step.

💬

Work & relationships

Navigating the workplace, managing communication, and understanding how ADHD affects your interactions with others.

❤️

Wellbeing & self-compassion

Building a kinder relationship with yourself, managing emotional dysregulation, and reducing overwhelm.

🎯

Goals & values

Getting clarity on what you actually want — and building a realistic path to get there that works with your brain.

🛠️

Practical tools

Exploring systems, apps, and strategies that can support your daily life — tailored to your needs and preferences.

What to expect from your first session

The first session is about getting to know each other properly and laying the groundwork for our work together. We will talk about what has brought you to coaching, what you are hoping to get out of it, and what is going on for you right now.

There is no assessment, no homework, and no expectations. By the end of the first session, we will have a clearer sense of your goals and how we will structure the rest of the block.

Many clients say the first session feels like a relief — just having a space to be heard and to start making sense of things, without judgement.

What actually happens in a session?

No two sessions are exactly alike — but here is a sense of what a typical 50-minute coaching session looks and feels like.

~5 min

Settle in

We start gently. No jumping straight in. A moment to arrive, decompress, and leave whatever came before at the door.

~10 min

Check in

How has the week been? What has shifted since we last spoke? Any wins — however small — are worth noticing.

~25 min

Main focus

The heart of the session. We work on whatever is most pressing — a goal, a challenge, a pattern you want to understand better, or a concrete plan to tackle something specific.

~7 min

Action & reflection

We identify any steps or experiments to try before the next session — always realistic, always chosen by you.

~3 min

Close

A brief check-out. How are you leaving? What are you taking with you? We note the next session date and you are on your way.

A few things worth knowing

  • You do not need to prepare anything in advance — just turn up.
  • The agenda is always yours. We follow what matters most to you on that day.
  • There is no pressure to have breakthroughs. Sometimes sessions are about untangling things. That counts.
  • Everything you share is confidential (see my Privacy Policy for the small number of exceptions).

Frequently asked questions

Do I need a formal ADHD diagnosis to access coaching?

Not necessarily. While many clients do have a formal diagnosis, I also work with people who are on the waiting list for assessment or who strongly identify with ADHD but have not yet been formally assessed. We can discuss your situation in the discovery call.

Is ADHD coaching the same as therapy?

No. Coaching is not a clinical or therapeutic service and does not treat mental health conditions. It is a practical, forward-looking partnership focused on strategies and goals. If you are experiencing significant mental health difficulties, I may recommend that you also access appropriate support alongside coaching.

What if I need to pause mid-block?

Life does not always go to plan — I understand that. If you need to pause your sessions, please just talk to me. We can look at options together, and I will always try to be as flexible as I reasonably can.

Where do sessions take place?

Sessions are in person at [location]. If you have accessibility needs or travel difficulties, please do mention this when you get in touch — I am happy to have a conversation about how we can make it work.

How do I know if coaching is right for me?

The free discovery call is exactly the right place to explore that question. You do not have to have a clear answer before you reach out. I will never pressure you into booking, and I will always be honest if I think another form of support might suit you better.
