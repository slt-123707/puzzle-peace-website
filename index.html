What is ADHD coaching?

ADHD coaching is a practical, forward-focused partnership. It is not therapy — it is a collaborative space where you explore how your ADHD brain works, identify what gets in the way, and develop personalised strategies to help you move forward in your life and work.

🧠

Understand

Gain a clearer picture of how your ADHD affects you specifically — your strengths, your challenges, and the patterns that have been holding you back.

🗺️

Strategise

Work with your coach to build practical, real-world tools and routines that fit your life — not someone else's idea of what "organised" looks like.

🌿

Thrive

Build momentum, self-compassion, and confidence — so that you can achieve what matters to you and feel more at peace with who you are.

What ADHD coaching is — and isn't

It helps to know what you are signing up for. Coaching is a specific kind of support — different from therapy, and different from advice.

Coaching is...

  • A practical, forward-focused partnership
  • Led by your goals and priorities
  • A space to explore, problem-solve, and plan
  • Built around how your brain actually works
  • Non-judgemental and entirely confidential
  • About building skills and strategies you keep
  • Grounded in the belief that you are capable

Coaching is not...

  • Therapy or counselling
  • A medical or clinical service
  • A route to diagnosis or assessment
  • Someone telling you what to do
  • About "fixing" you — you do not need fixing
  • A substitute for medication or mental health care
  • Only for people who have everything figured out

If you are experiencing significant mental health difficulties, coaching works best alongside — not instead of — appropriate clinical support. I will always be honest with you if I think another form of help might serve you better.

Could coaching help you?

You might benefit from ADHD coaching if you are…

  • ✦   Struggling with time blindness and deadlines
  • ✦   Feeling constantly overwhelmed or disorganised
  • ✦   Difficulty starting tasks (even ones you want to do)
  • ✦   Emotional dysregulation and rejection sensitivity
  • ✦   Wanting to understand yourself better
  • ✦   Ready to make a change but unsure where to start

You do not need to have everything figured out before you reach out. That is exactly what coaching is for.
